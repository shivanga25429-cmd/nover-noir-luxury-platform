

# NOVER NOIR — Premium Perfume E-Commerce Website

## Brand Identity
- **Primary background:** Deep dark green (#0B3D2E)
- **Gold accents:** #C6A75E for buttons, borders, and highlights
- **Typography:** Cinzel/Playfair Display for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- **Aesthetic:** Tom Ford / Dior inspired — dramatic spacing, cinematic feel, minimal luxury

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Homepage
- **Hero Section** — Full-screen dramatic hero with "NOVER NOIR" in large serif type, tagline "Luxury in Every Drop.", gold "Shop Now" CTA with hover glow, subtle parallax/fade-in animation
- **Featured Perfumes** — 3-4 product cards with elegant hover effects, product image, name, price (₹200–₹1000), and "Add to Cart" button
- **Why Choose Us** — Three pillars: Long Lasting, Affordable Luxury, Premium Packaging — with icons and fade-in animations
- **Customer Testimonials** — Carousel of reviews with star ratings, minimal elegant styling
- **Final CTA Banner** — Bold statement with gold "Shop Now" button

### 2. Shop Page
- Product grid with clean cards (image, name, price, Add to Cart)
- Filter/sort options (price range, fragrance family)
- Smooth scroll and staggered fade-in on product cards

### 3. Product Detail Page
- Large hero product image with dramatic dark background
- Product name, price, description
- Fragrance notes breakdown (Top, Middle, Base) with elegant visual layout
- "Add to Cart" gold button
- Customer reviews section at bottom

### 4. About Us Page
- Brand story with cinematic typography and spacing
- Brand values and mission
- Visual imagery section

### 5. Contact Page
- Clean contact form (name, email, message)
- Brand contact info and social links

---

## Design & UX Details
- Smooth scroll animations and fade-in effects throughout
- Gold buttons with subtle glow on hover
- Fully mobile responsive
- Cart functionality with item count indicator in navbar
- Sticky navigation bar with brand logo and gold accents
- Clean, modern footer with links and social icons

---

## Technical Notes
- Frontend-only with mock product data (ready for backend/payment integration later)
- SEO-friendly page titles and meta structure
- Fast loading with optimized layout
- Cart state managed in React context (local, no backend needed initially)
